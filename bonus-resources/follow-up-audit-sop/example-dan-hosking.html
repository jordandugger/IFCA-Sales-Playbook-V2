02 — Constraint Diagnosis
What's Actually Blocking You
Ranked by priority — P1 is what we fix first
Cost of inaction — based on your £20K/mo goal
£54,000+
At your stated £20K goal, every month you stay at £2K is roughly £18K in forgone income. At 3 months of delay: £54,000 left on the table. Even at a conservative £5K interim target, you're leaving £9,000/month behind. The referral model that got you here cannot get you to £20K.
P1
Offer & Pricing — 3–4× Below Market, No Locked Niche
£400 for 12 weeks is £33/month. Market rate is £150–200/month minimum. Your niche is also undefined — you're coaching a wide mix of clients with no clear avatar. Without a specific offer and a repositioned price, ads, content, and sales all point at the wrong target.
IFCA Fix: Week one — AI Offer Optimization Scorecard. Lock your avatar, name your mechanism, reprice to £150–200/month, rewrite your IG bio. You go into week two knowing exactly what you're selling and to who.
P2
Lead Generation — No System, Pure Referral Dependency
Every client has come from someone you personally know. That's not scalable. You need strangers coming to you on a predictable schedule — not just people who happen to remember you exist. Without a lead engine, revenue will always feel like a rollercoaster.
IFCA Fix: Affinity launch to warm network (3–7 clients before touching ads) → content calendar + AI content engine → DM automation → paid ads when the offer converts organically. IFCA covers your first month of ad spend (~£300).
P2
Backend Systems — Running a Business on Nothing
EverFit for delivery. ChatGPT for meal plans. No CRM, no contracts, no payment processing, no automated follow-up. When a lead reaches out, there's no structured path from interested to paying. This becomes the bottleneck the moment volume increases.
IFCA Fix: Impact AI (done-for-you CRM) installed in the first 7 days — contracts, scheduling, payments, email/SMS automations. All in one place. EverFit stays for delivery; this handles everything else.
P3
Sales Framework — Good Instincts, No Structure Yet
You've done very few formal coaching sales calls. You know the questions to ask but don't have a repeatable framework for moving someone from interested to signed. This will surface as a bottleneck when lead volume increases.
IFCA Fix: Sales coach provides personalized call feedback. Pre-qualification sequences so only ready buyers reach you. Scripts built for fitness-specific discovery.
OK
Delivery & Retention — Your Actual Coaching Is Strong
20 clients, strong retention, real results. This is the hardest thing to teach and you already have it. The IFCA Trainerize library fills the EverFit nutrition gap and gives you a premium delivery system from day one — without rebuilding what's already working.
