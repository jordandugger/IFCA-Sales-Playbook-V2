Closer SOP · Follow-Up System

Follow-Up Loom Audit

Re-engage prospects who didn't close, no-showed, or went quiet. Build a personalized business audit and walk it through on a Loom — turn cold follow-ups into clarity-driven re-opens. Takes about 20–30 minutes from transcript to Loom sent.

What This Is
A personalized audit replaces a generic "just checking in" text.

When a prospect doesn't close, a generic follow-up won't get them back on a call. This system builds a personalized business audit for each prospect that shows you diagnosed their actual constraints — not just their surface objection — and gives you a structured Loom script to walk through it.

Claude builds a two-tab HTML file you download and open in your browser. Tab 1 (Prospect View) is what you screen-share during the Loom. Tab 2 (Closer Intel) is your internal prep: objection bank, Loom script outline, and follow-up prep notes. You never share the file — you record a Loom over Tab 1 and send the prospect the Loom link only.

Section 3 · Loom Structure
The 5 Beats
1
Open
30 sec
"Hey [Name] — [you] here. Wanted to send something over before our next call."
2
Cost of Inaction
45 sec
Pull up the red COI number. "Every month at [the job / current situation] is $X you're not making."
3
Constraint Walkthrough
2 min
Walk P1 → P2 → P3 constraint cards using their exact words from the call.
4
Proof Bridge
45 sec
Anchor to their prior success or the results they already got.
5
Solutions + Close
1 min
Reference the Solutions table. Give one CTA — follow-up date, or "text me if another program reaches out first." Not both.
⚠ Hard Rule
Do not re-sell on the Loom. The Loom's job is clarity and certainty — not a second pitch. Show them what's blocking them, show them what IFCA builds, and stop. When the prospect watches it, the follow-up call should feel like paperwork.

Immediately after the call Log outcome in HubSpot. Update lead status, add call note, set follow-up task.

Within 24 hours Submit to Claude, build the audit, review Closer Intel, record the Loom.

Section 7
FAQ
Do I send the HTML file to the prospect?

No. Never. The Closer Intel tab contains internal diagnosis and objection decoding that is not for prospect eyes. Send the Loom link only.

What if I don't have the Grain transcript?

Paste your call notes instead. If you only have HubSpot + setter notes, Claude can still build a partial audit. A partial audit is better than no follow-up.

How long should the Loom be?

5–7 minutes. Under 5 feels cursory. Over 8 and most prospects won't finish it. Follow the 5-beat structure and you'll land in range.

What if they watch it and still don't respond?

Wait 48 hours then send one follow-up text: "Hey [Name] — any questions before [date]?" After that, move to General Follow-Up in HubSpot with a 2-week task. Don't chase.

Can I use this before an initial call?

Yes — with the right inputs. The audit needs context to produce a useful output. If you have a setter or triage call recorded, a thorough application, social presence, and HubSpot notes — you can absolutely run this pre-call. The diagnosis quality scales directly with input quality.

Until then, deploy this pre-call only with prospects where you already have substantial context — a previous call, deep setter notes, or a documented triage conversation. Don't run it on application-only data — the output won't earn the time.
